## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### Student Pack Not Approved?
- Make sure you used a `.edu` or university email (e.g., `s123456@nutech.edu.pk`)
- If no university email: upload a clear photo of your student ID card
- Check approval status at [education.github.com](https://education.github.com)
- Approval usually takes 1‚Äì7 days

### DataCamp Access Expired?
- The GitHub Student Pack gives 2 months free DataCamp
- After expiry: DataCamp has a **free tier** with limited courses ‚Äî use it for basic practice
- Re-apply for Student Pack after your 2-month trial ends if needed

### Azure Credits Running Low?
- Free services (VM B1s, Blob Storage, SQL) won't consume credits
- Only pay-as-you-go resources consume credits ‚Äî stop VMs when not in use
- Go to Cost Management in Azure Portal to monitor spending
